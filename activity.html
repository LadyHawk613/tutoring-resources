Rounding — The Big Idea

Which neighbor is it closer to?

Rounding just asks: which number is this closer to? The number line makes it visual.

3.7 midpoint: 3.75 3.8
3.748 — is it closer to 3.7 or 3.8?
The Rounding Rule

Look at the digit to the RIGHT of where you're rounding.

5 or more → round UP  |  4 or less → keep it the SAME

For 3.748 rounded to the nearest tenth: look at the hundredths digit → 4 → that's 4 or less, so the tenths digit (7) stays → 3.7
